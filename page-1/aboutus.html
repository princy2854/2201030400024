ABOUT US

Join a community of compassionate skilled individuals making a difference, one challenge at a time. Whether you're a coder, artist, runner, or puzzle-solver, your skills can help fund projects and organizations making real-world impacts. Take on a challenge, showcase your talent, and contribute to your chosen charity with SkillGive.

HOW IT WORKS?

Step 1: "Select a Challenge - Browse through a diverse list of challenges ranging from physical activities, coding marathons, artistic creations, to educational quizzes."
Step 2: "Showcase Your Skills - Complete the challenge using your unique talents and share your accomplishment within our community."
Step 3: "Donate and Make an Impact - Upon completing a challenge, contribute an amount of your choice to a charity linked with the challenge. Watch as your skills translate into tangible aid for those in need."
